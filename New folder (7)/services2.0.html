Architectural Services

Our highly skilled Architects designs exquisite designs and supervise their implementation. Our team follows the below criteria of design development for seamless service delivery, involving our clients at each stage to guarantee their satisfaction

    Schematic Design: Establish the conceptual design, scale and relationship among spaces. Design Development: Refine the design and prepare plans, elevations, building sections and typical details. Contract Documents: Prepare detailed working drawings and specifications for construction. Bidding and Negotiation: Solicit bids from contractors and negotiate the contract for construction. Contract Administration: Observe construction to assure the project is built according to the drawings and specifications.
